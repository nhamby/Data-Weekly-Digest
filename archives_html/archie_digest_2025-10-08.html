Your private data isn't as private as you think

Source: Werd.io

Law enforcement increasingly accesses private data, often without warrants, using third-party brokers. Private entities exploit legal loopholes, like data brokers, to acquire sensitive user information, raising privacy concerns. Individuals can mitigate risks by using privacy-focused tools, opting out of data sharing where possible, and advocating for stronger data protection laws.
