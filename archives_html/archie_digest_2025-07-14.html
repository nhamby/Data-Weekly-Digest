Nebraska sues GM over data collection , says customer info was unlawfully sold

Source: klkntv.com

Nebraska is suing GM, alleging it unlawfully collected and sold customer driving data. The lawsuit claims GM enrolled drivers in OnStar Smart Driver without explicit consent, affecting over 685,000 Nebraskans. GM then shared this sensitive driving data with third-party insurance companies, potentially increasing premiums unfairly for drivers.
