Navigating Modern Data Architecture: DW, Lakehouse, and Lakebase Explained

Source: Sqltechblog.com

Data warehouses offer structured data analysis but struggle with unstructured and semi-structured types, limiting analytical scope. Data lakehouses combine warehouse and lake benefits, achieving 2-10x faster query performance through optimized storage. Lakebases represent the newest architecture, promising real-time data ingestion and analysis with ACID transactions, although deployment complexity remains.
